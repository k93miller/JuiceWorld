About

At Juice Laundry, we believe wellness starts from within — and it tastes amazing, too. Born from a passion for clean, nourishing fuel, Juice Laundry was created to offer the freshest, most delicious cold-pressed juices, smoothies, and plant-based blends made with real, whole ingredients. No additives. No shortcuts. Just pure, vibrant nutrition in every sip. We’re here to make healthy choices easier, more accessible, and way more fun. Whether you’re grabbing a green juice to kick-start your day or refueling with a protein-packed smoothie after a workout, our menu is designed to energize your body and refresh your spirit..

History

  • March 2025

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our humble beginnings? Well, it all started when we found ourselves knee-deep in a website project—somewhere between 'This is impossible' and 'Do we really need this grade?' We powered through with a mix of confusion, late nights, and more coffee than we'd care to admit.

  • Early April 2025

    The Legacy is Born

    And just like that, a legacy was born—mostly out of sheer determination, questionable design choices, and the hope that we would somehow pass this class.

  • Late April 2025

    The Transition

    As the project wrapped up, our smoothie empire quietly folded—turns out, blending pixels wasn’t quite as profitable as we thought, so we moved on to bigger and better things.
